Live Virtual Property Viewings in Cape Town

We send a trusted local agent to view properties on your behalf — live, honest, and anywhere in Cape Town.

Buy or Rent Property in Cape Town — Without Being Here

Our Virtual Property Viewing service is designed for international buyers, investors, and digital nomads who cannot attend property viewings in person. A professional local agent visits the property and connects with you via a live video call.

During the call, the agent walks through the home in real time, shows the surrounding streets, answers your questions, and shares honest insights about the neighbourhood, pricing, and overall suitability — helping you make confident decisions from anywhere in the world.

Perfect for international buyers, investors, and digital nomads relocating to Cape Town.

Live Video Property Viewing
A real agent walks through the property on a live call, showing layout, finishes, lighting, and surroundings — just like being there.

Neighbourhood & Area Insight
We show streets, nearby amenities, lifestyle factors, and give real local insight on safety, convenience, and value.

Professional Agent Feedback
Get honest, on-the-ground opinions — including red flags, pricing insights, and whether the property truly fits your goals.

Alternative Property Suggestions
If the property isn’t right, we suggest better or similar options nearby — saving you time and avoiding costly mistakes.
